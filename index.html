01  ·  Thesis

Psychedelic phenomenology is where two disciplines meet.

It is the careful study of what an experience is actually like — coded, compared across many people, and never flattened into a checklist or discarded as noise. Phenomenology is the study of experience from the inside: not the receptor or the behaviour, but the way a situation actually shows up for the person living it. For a science of the mind, that first-person account is not decoration. It is data.

A single dose can bring serenity in one session and terror in the next, and that variability is not random. It is shaped by mindset and environment — set and setting — as much as by pharmacology. How context steers the experience is the open problem this work is built around. only_noise is small, slow, and unaffiliated by choice.

Ozora Festival  ·  Hungary  ·  46.9°N
